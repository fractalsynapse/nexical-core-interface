Getting Started with the Nexical Knowledge Explorer

Nexical Core is an open AI platform that accelerates business productivity across your organization focused on data privacy, auditability and security, and research efficiency with your internal data and systems, while being cost effective and avoiding vendor lock-in.

You can get started with Nexical in 4 easy steps...

1. Create Teams

The first step in using Nexical Knowledge Explorer is to set up one or more teams.

All documents, projects, and research material are accessible only to active team members.

At any time you are active in a single team, which may be changed by selecting another team in the team selector at the top right of the page.

2. Import Document Libraries

The next step is to import business document libraries for use in integrated research activities.

When you upload documents to the Nexical engine they are processed and prepared for AI analysis over time.

The core platform supports manual document uploads but bulk and automated imports can easily be added by our team.

3. Manage Projects

All AI research artifacts and human notes are grouped into projects.

Projects allow you to configure AI systems and link document libraries across all related research activities.

Projects when combined with tag filtering can provide a simple and streamlined knowledge management system.

4. Conduct Research

Once we have a project we can start querying the referenced documents and taking notes.

Nexical Core seeks to combine human research and machine research into a common searchable knowledge base over time.

As more AI generated artifacts and notes are added the AI incorporates that information for new research requests.

AI generated artifacts and information and team notes can be tagged and easily filtered within the currently active project.

Interface with the API

When you need more capabilities than our web interface or you need to integrate with external systems, you can build off our REST API.

All Nexical Knowledge Explorer data and capabilities can be accessed and performed through an easy to use OpenAPI compatible REST API.

Our API is well documented and you can experiment with it through the Nexical Core Knowledge Explorer user interface.

Manage your Account

You can easily edit your profile information, verified emails, passwords, and API tokens.

When you need to modify anything relating to your user account you can find all user actions in the sidebar menu on the left.
